I'm Kalyn, An Aspiring UX Designer.

I am a student currently pursing my BA in Web Design and Development. When I was 19, I joined the Navy as an Electronics Technician. At the end of my 8 years, I decided that it was time for a new adventure. With a love for photography and technology, I knew I had to find a career that would build my technical skills and let me be creative. So, the last 3 years I have been using the skills I acquired in the military (analytical thinking, persistence, discipline) to learn front-end development and UX Design.

Through my education and hands-on projects, I am continually refining my skills and staying current with UX/UI trends to deliver exceptional experiences for end users. I have a passion for learning that drives me to continue building my knowledge further than college courses such as the Google's UX/UI Design Course and Digital Marketing Course. I’m excited to grow as a web designer, using creativity and user-centered design to bring ideas to life online.
